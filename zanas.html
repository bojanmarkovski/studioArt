За нас

Born in Argentina from Spanish and French origins, Manuel Otero Martí has ​​always looked beyond the boundaries, creating first a strong bond with Brazil and moving soon to the US to complete his studies.

After working as an architect for almost 10 years, Manuel Otero Martí arrives in Italy to obtain a second degree in Business Administration at an American University in Rome.

His personal and multifaceted training continues with specialized studies in Forensic Psychiatry at La Sapienza, while professionally working in the financial sector.

After the Roman experience Manuel Otero Martí obtains an MBA at the University of Bologna Business School undertaking a successful career in the luxury business.

Notwithstanding, life’s experience is much more than a simple curriculum: it is made of unforeseen challenges, hopeless and uncertain tunnels, goals that seem unreachable.

This has been the true formation of Manuel Otero Martí: a path that, at a certain point, finally led him to understand the true reason of his existence, returning to his essence.
